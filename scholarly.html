Introduction

Business Admin is an web application designed to be helpfull for administration of business, in special for startups.

Purpose

Busines Admin is an web application thought to be a good tool for administration of a small business. As a lot of business had issues with the administration, either they forgot how much quantity of a product they have or they can not manage well the money from their business. The user can be a simple client, an employee or the administrator. The client can place a order. The employee will recieve the order, and the administrator will have full visibility of it. The application comes from the experience my friends have to manage their business. At the moment the application will be free, but it will be devoloped a premium version for more functionality.

Conventions

The admin has the ability to manage the stocks of their products, the money the have, the money they spend for investion, salaries and others and the money they win. To be more complex, the employee will be able to register on the app and help the administration by providing information about the stock which was made, what they need for their work. This will help the business to reduce the loss.

Description

Perspective

The application is design to help small business to reduce their loss and help them grow with a good managing of their money, stock and ohers. For the begining, the app will be free, but a more complex version will be designed for premium users.

Functions

The Business Admin come with the function of administration all the money from the business, manage their orders of products or services and also help the administrator to know what he needs to buy for a proper development of the work.

Operating environment

The program is developted with Django, HTML templates, CSS and SQL.

Design constraints

As business adminsitration is a very complex task, the application is still not able to do all the task yet.

The good administration of someone's businees is the main goal. The app manage businees money, clients, orders and also employees.
